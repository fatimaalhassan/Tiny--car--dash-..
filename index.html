📜 How to Play

  • Choose your favorite car from the selection menu.
  • Press Start to begin the race.
  • Colored buttons will appear below the track.
  • If you press the button that matches your car’s color, your speed increases by +1.
  • If you press the wrong color, your speed decreases.
  • The winner is the car that goes the farthest before the timer ⏰ runs out.
